About Ironclad

Two decades of building landmark structures, forging lasting partnerships, and setting the standard for construction excellence.

Our Story

Built on Trust,
Driven by Excellence

Founded in Chicago in 2004 by civil engineer Thomas Harrington, Ironclad Construction began with a simple mission: deliver construction projects that stand the test of time — in quality, in durability, and in client satisfaction.

Over two decades, we've grown from a regional contractor to a nationally recognized firm, completing over 340 projects valued at more than $2 billion across commercial, residential, and infrastructure sectors.

Today, our team of 150+ professionals operates with the same relentless attention to quality that defined our very first project — every foundation laid, every beam set, every structure delivered represents our unwavering commitment to the craft.

Our Mission

To deliver construction projects of the highest quality — on time, within budget, and with an unwavering commitment to safety — while building lasting relationships with our clients, partners, and communities.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted construction company in America — recognized not just for the structures we build, but for the integrity, innovation, and impact we bring to every project we undertake.
